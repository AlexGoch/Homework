Our Cats

We currently have four cats (with no immediate plans for more). The oldest is Baby, who is ~13 years old. Then there are the twins Walter and Eevee, who are about a year and a half old. The youngest cat is Jasper, who is barely a year. Baby was a shelter cat that Maddie got shortly after we started dating. Walter and Eevee are both barn cats from Maddie's family's farm, who we took home with us when they were 8 weeks old. Jasper was an impulsive adoption, as we had found out that a mutual friend was giving away kittens.

Baby

  • Loves Maddie
  • Does not love Alex
  • Hobbies include sitting in windows and scratching cardboard
  • Also does not love Walter

Walter

  • Loves everybody
  • Is the Golden King who rules over everyone in the apartment
  • Hobbies include sleeping all day and slapping the other cats in the face
  • Always occupies the highest point in whichever room he is in

Eevee

  • Likes to sit on Maddie's chest and crush her
  • Is the heaviest cat, weighing in at roughly 15 pounds
  • Hobbies include forcing doors open with her face and stealing hair ties
  • Likes to yell at you if she isn't getting enough attention

Jasper

  • Is the weirdest cat
  • Has an irrational obsession with one specific blanket
  • Hobbies include birdwatching and hanging out with Alex
  • Only likes to eat if he is being pet
